# BI 471 Population Ecology



Coursework and final project for BI 471. Uses R and R Markdown to simulate population dynamics, apply demographic models, and analyze species interactions. All reports rendered as HTML via R Markdown.

---

## Final project — Southern Resident Killer Whale Climate-Toxin Model

> Does climate-driven Chinook salmon decline accelerate SRKW population collapse by amplifying internal PCB exposure through blubber mobilization — and at what rate of prey decline does this toxin feedback become the decisive driver of extinction risk?

- Stage-structured stochastic matrix model across 4 life stages
- Toxin sub-model tracking PCB accumulation, blubber mobilization, and maternal transfer
- Climate scenarios from IPCC SSP projections (RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5)
- Population of ~73 critically endangered individuals as of 2024
